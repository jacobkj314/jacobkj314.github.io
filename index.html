Hello!

I am Jacob Johnson, currently working on my M.S. in Computer Engineering at Weber State University in Ogden, Utah. I am most interested in Digital Signal Processing and Embedded Systems. This Summer (2026), I will also be teaching at Weber!

I graduated with a B.A. in Linguistics from the University of Utah, and for a time after that I remained there as a Computer Science PhD student, advised by Ana Marasović of UtahNLP, with whom I researched the application of theoretical/experimental linguistics principles to natural language processing.

Outside of school I enjoy tinkering with computers, reading books, and swimming. I'm happily married to my lovely wife Helen.
